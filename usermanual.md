# DriftDater – User Manual

## Getting Started
To use DriftDater, open your browser and go to the app URL. You will be greeted with the home page which gives an overview of the app's features.


## Registration
Click "Log In" in the navigation bar and then click "Sign Up" to create a new account. Enter your email address and a password, then click Sign Up. You will be redirected to the login page once registered successfully.


## Logging In
Click "Log In" in the navigation bar. Enter your registered email and password and click the Login button. You will be redirected to the dashboard upon successful login.


## Creating a Profile
After logging in, navigate to your profile page and fill in the required fields — full name, age, bio, location, and interests. You can also upload a profile picture and set your profile visibility to public or private. Click Save when done.


## Discovering Matches
On the dashboard you will see a list of potential matches sorted by match score. Each profile displays the user's name, age, bio, location and interests. Click Like to like a profile or Pass to skip them.


## Matching
When two users both like each other a mutual match is created. You can view all your matches in the Matches section of the app.


## Messaging
Click on a match to open a conversation. Type your message in the text box and click Send. Messages are saved and you can view your conversation history at any time.
Logging Out
Click Logout in the navigation bar to end your session securely.
